About Red Maple Homestay

Your Peaceful Retreat in the Hills

Nestled in the beautiful village of Naya Busty, Sonada, Red Maple Homestay offers stunning mountain views, fresh Himalayan air, and authentic local hospitality.

Wake up to birdsong, rolling clouds, pine forests and the peaceful atmosphere of Darjeeling's hills. Whether you are travelling with family, friends or as a solo explorer, we provide a comfortable and memorable stay.

Nearby Attractions

🚂 Sonada Railway Station

Part of the famous Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, a UNESCO World Heritage treasure.

🙏 Sonada Monastery

A peaceful Buddhist monastery featuring beautiful Tibetan architecture.

🌈 Indreni Falls

A hidden natural waterfall surrounded by lush greenery and fresh mountain air.

✝️ Holy Grotto

A beautiful religious site and peaceful place for visitors.

☸️ Sacred Stupas

Traditional Buddhist stupas located near the monastery grounds.

🌲 Pine Forest Views

Enjoy spectacular views of Himalayan pine forests directly from the homestay.
