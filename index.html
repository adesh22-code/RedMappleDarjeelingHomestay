Your Peaceful Retreat in the Hills

Nestled in the cozy hamlet of Naya Busty, Sonada, Red Maple Homestay offers breathtaking panoramic views of rolling clouds, lush green terraces, and deep pine woods. Escape the hustle of city life and wake up to pristine mountain air and traditional local hospitality.

Homestay Amenities

🏔️ Scenic Views

Enjoy stunning front garden landscapes and panoramic misty rooftop vistas directly facing the pine ridges.

🍲 Home-Cooked Meals

Savor authentic local organic dishes freshly prepared with love and organic hill ingredients.

🚶 Guided Village Walks

Immerse yourself in mountain life with guided trails through the local community and nearby natural wonders.

Nearby Attractions in Sonada

🚂 Historic Sonada Railway Station

Part of the legendary Darjeeling Himalayan Railway (DHR) UNESCO World Heritage site, built in 1881.

🙏 Samdrup Darjay Choling Monastery

A beautiful Buddhist monastery featuring stunning traditional Tibetan architecture, vibrant murals, and sacred stupas.

🌈 Indreni Falls

A pristine rocky waterfall hidden within nature where you can catch spectacular sights of natural rainbows dance in the mist.

✝️ The Holy Grotto

A beautifully constructed religious shrine with life-sized golden statues nestled peacefully against the rocky slopes.
